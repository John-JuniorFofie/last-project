About Agro Link

Connecting farmers directly to buyers for a sustainable agricultural future in Ghana

Our Story

Agro Link was founded in 2023 by a team of agricultural enthusiasts and tech innovators who recognized the challenges faced by local farmers in Ghana. We noticed that while small-scale farmers produced high-quality crops, they often struggled to find reliable markets and fair prices for their produce.

At the same time, buyers including restaurants, markets, and individual consumers were having difficulty accessing fresh, locally grown products directly from farmers. The existing supply chain was inefficient, with too many middlemen reducing farmers' profits while increasing costs for buyers.

What began as a small pilot project connecting 20 farmers in the Ashanti Region with buyers in Kumasi has now grown into a nationwide platform serving over 5,000 farmers and 10,000 buyers across all 16 regions of Ghana.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Ghana's agricultural sector by creating direct connections between farmers and buyers through innovative technology, ensuring fair prices for farmers and fresh, affordable produce for buyers while promoting sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's leading agricultural marketplace where every farmer has equal access to profitable markets, every buyer can easily source fresh local produce, and where technology drives efficiency, transparency and growth across the entire agricultural value chain.
